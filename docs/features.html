All Features

Packed with everything you need

RecordIt brings professional recording and collaboration tools together in one Alvonia UI app.

⏺ Screen Recording

4K Ultra HD Recording

Record at resolutions from 480p up to 4K Ultra HD (3840×2160) with frame rates of 15, 24, 30, or 60 fps. Hardware-accelerated encoding via GPU.

Multiple Capture Sources

Choose to capture your entire screen, a specific monitor, a single application window, or a custom region.

Audio Mixing

Record microphone input, system audio, or both simultaneously. Independent volume controls and noise suppression.

Webcam Overlay

Add a picture-in-picture webcam overlay. Customizable size, position, and shape (circular or rectangular).

Multiple Output Formats

Save recordings as MP4 (H.264/H.265), WebM (VP9), MKV, or AVI. Configurable bitrate and codec settings.

Countdown Timer

Optional 3-second countdown before recording starts so you can position your windows before capture begins.

✏️ Whiteboard (MS Teams-Style)

Rich Drawing Tools

Pen, highlighter, eraser, and a full set of shapes: lines, rectangles, ellipses, and triangles. Adjustable brush sizes.

Text & Sticky Notes

Add text annotations anywhere on the canvas. Create colorful sticky notes for ideas, reminders, and feedback.

Color Palette

10-color quick-access palette plus a full color picker. Custom colors saved per session.

Undo / Redo

Full undo/redo history. Keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+Z, Ctrl+Y) for fast corrections.

Participants Panel

See who is collaborating on the whiteboard in real-time. Participant cursors and presence indicators just like MS Teams.

Export to PNG

Save your whiteboard as a high-resolution PNG image. Works on both the WinUI 3 and Electron apps.

📁 Recording Library

Organized Grid View

Browse all your recordings and whiteboards in a clean thumbnail grid. Sort by date, name, or file size.

Search & Filter

Quickly find recordings by name. Filter by type (video or whiteboard) with one click.

Storage Stats

Track total storage usage, recording counts, and disk space at a glance from the library dashboard.

🎨 Alvonia UI Design System

Dark & Light Themes

First-class dark mode (default) and light mode. Toggle instantly from the title bar. Respects system preference.

Cross-Platform Parity

Identical UI between WinUI 3 and Electron. Same colors, typography, spacing, and interactions everywhere.

Custom Title Bar

Frameless window with a custom title bar showing the app logo, recording indicator, theme toggle, and window controls.
